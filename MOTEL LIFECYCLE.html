Discover the comfort

With one semester of college under my belt, I've come to realize how amazing it is to have the opportunity to receive an education. I have a plethora of resources at my disposal and I feel as though I am on the right path at Marywood. However, I did not single handedly get to this position. I have to say that I have lived a comfortable life. As far back as I can remember, my parents were the ones who gave me all I have. They both have worked so hard to make my life as easy as it could be. From a young age, they instilled values in me that have molded me into the person I am today. Work ethic, gratefulness, compassion and strength are only a few qualities that my parent's have strived to impart to me.
